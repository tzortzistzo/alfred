About Alfred

Your travel paperwork, sorted.

Alfred was built for one simple reason: navigating visa requirements, entry forms, and travel authorisations is unnecessarily complicated. Whether you are a seasoned traveller or planning your first international trip, knowing exactly what documents you need — and when to obtain them — should not require hours of research across government websites.

Alfred does that work for you. Enter your passport nationality, your destination, and your purpose of travel. Alfred returns a clear, structured summary of everything you need to know before you leave — visa requirements, electronic travel authorisations, mandatory arrival forms, passport validity rules, and transit conditions.

How Alfred works

Alfred combines a curated database of travel rules covering 199 countries with an AI reasoning layer that handles edge cases, less common routes, and purpose-specific requirements. Common routes are answered instantly from the database at no cost. For less common combinations, Alfred consults its AI engine — which is then cached, so subsequent travellers asking the same question receive an instant, free response.

Alfred does not search the internet on every query. Results are deterministic, structured, and cached — making the system fast, consistent, and increasingly accurate over time as more routes are learned and verified.

Our partnership with Sherpa°

For travellers who require a visa or electronic authorisation, Alfred has partnered with Sherpa° — the world’s leading travel requirements platform, trusted by airlines, travel agencies, and millions of travellers worldwide. When Alfred identifies that a visa or authorisation is required, it provides a direct link to Sherpa°’s application platform, where travellers can apply securely, quickly, and with confidence.

Sherpa° processes applications across hundreds of visa types and destinations, offering 24/7 support and official-source data. The partnership means Alfred users move seamlessly from understanding their requirements to fulfilling them — without leaving a trusted environment.

Built for a moving world

International travel is growing faster than ever. Remote work, digital nomadism, global education, and cross-border commerce mean that more people than ever need accurate, real-time travel document guidance. Governments update visa policies frequently — sometimes overnight. Alfred is designed to reflect this reality: a living system that learns, updates, and serves travellers wherever they are going.

Alfred is currently in Beta. We are continuously expanding our coverage, improving accuracy, and refining the experience based on real usage. If you encounter incorrect information or have feedback, we welcome it — your input directly improves Alfred for every traveller who follows.

A note on accuracy

Alfred provides guidance based on the best available information and should be used as a starting point for travel planning. Entry requirements can change without notice. Always confirm final requirements with the official embassy, consulate, or government source of your destination country before travelling.
